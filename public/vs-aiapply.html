Quality vs Quantity: The Real Debate

This is the core philosophical difference between the two tools.

🎯

TailorCV's Approach

Apply to fewer jobs, but make every application feel like it was written specifically for that company. Research shows tailored applications get 2–3× more interview callbacks than generic ones. TailorCV is built for this · every bullet point, every skill, every line is adapted to the job description.

⚡

AIApply's Approach

Apply to as many jobs as possible as fast as possible. This works if you're in a high-volume market and have a strong base resume. The risk: recruiters can tell when applications are generic, and ATS systems may flag templated content.
