How it works

From job posting to perfect resume
in five simple steps

No lengthy setup. No confusing dashboards. Just sign up and go.

1

Pick your career profile

Choose from 18 professional niches - from Software Engineer to Nurse Manager to Marketing Director. TailorCV adapts its AI for your field.

2

Upload your existing resume

Drop in your current .docx or PDF resume. TailorCV reads it and uses it as the foundation for your tailored version.

3

Paste the job description

Copy the full job posting and paste it in. The AI analyzes what the employer is looking for and aligns your resume with it.

4

Choose your template

Pick from 13 templates - TailorCV even recommends the top 3 best-fit templates for your specific career profile.

5

Download & apply

Export your polished .docx resume, ATS score report, cover letter, and skills gap analysis - then hit that Apply button.
